The promises. Not negotiable, not configurable.

No ads. No telemetry. No tracking. No profiling. Nothing sent to any server.

What the promises mean

No adsEver, in any product. Advertising is the business model we exist to escape.
No telemetryThe software does not phone home. Not even "anonymously".
No trackingNo profiles, no analytics, no third parties.
Nothing sentCore protections run locally, on your machine.

We do not claim Norway is a safe jurisdiction, because it is not one. Our privacy comes from collecting nothing, not from where the association sits. What does not exist cannot be handed over.

Honest positioningNorway takes part in international intelligence cooperation. We do not pretend otherwise.

Where we stand

The association was founded on 26 June 2026 in Fredrikstad and is registered in the Norwegian register of legal entities. Right now we are building the institution: governance, transparency, financial readiness.

The products come after, and not from us alone. Security-critical software will be written and reviewed by funded engineers who put their names on the work. We are seeking funding for exactly that, and we promise nothing we cannot keep.

The name

Batin (باطن) means the inner, the hidden. The name carries the whole idea: what is private and innermost in a person should stay hidden and protected.

The mark is several things at once: a moon and a star, a hidden letter B, a keyhole, and a human being with open, lifted hands. Freedom.

باطنAl-Batin, The Hidden, is one of the names of God in Islam.
Why "dignity, not just privacy"?

Today's software makes its money by watching and exploiting people. We build the opposite: tools that take nothing from you and sell nothing on. Privacy is the technique. Dignity is the reason.
