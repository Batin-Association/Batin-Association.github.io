Why Batin exists

Most software profits by watching you. We build the opposite.

Today's software makes its money by observing and exploiting people. Batin is the refusal of that: tools that take nothing from you and sell nothing on.

The promises. Not negotiable, not configurable.

No ads. No telemetry. No tracking. No profiling. Nothing sent to any server.

١

What the promises mean

No adsEver, in any product. Advertising is the business model we exist to escape.
No telemetryThe software does not phone home. Not even "anonymously".
No trackingNo profiles, no analytics, no third parties.
No profilingWe do not build a picture of who you are.
Nothing sentCore protections run locally, on your machine.
٢

Honest positioning

We do not claim Norway is a safe jurisdiction, because it is not one. Norway takes part in international intelligence cooperation, and we will not pretend otherwise.

Our privacy does not come from where the association sits. It comes from collecting almost nothing. What does not exist cannot be handed over, subpoenaed, leaked or sold.

PlainlyPrivacy by design, not by geography.
٣

Dignity, not just privacy

Privacy is the technique. Dignity is the reason. We treat the person at the other end of the screen as a human being to be protected, not a resource to be harvested. That is the whole point, and it is why the promises above can never be traded away.
