The name

Batin means the hidden. That is the whole idea.

The name carries the mission in one word: what is private and innermost in a person should stay hidden and protected.

١

Batin, باطن

Batin (باطن) means the inner, the hidden. It is the opposite of what is exposed and on show. We chose it because privacy, at its core, is about protecting the part of a person that is not for public consumption.

باطنAl-Batin, The Hidden, is one of the names of God in Islam.
٢

The mark

The mark is deliberately several things at once, read differently depending on where you look:

  • A moon and a star.
  • A hidden letter B.
  • A keyhole, for the private and the hidden.
  • A human being with open, lifted hands. Freedom.
